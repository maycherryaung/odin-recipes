Tomato and Tofu Miso Soup

This is a vegan miso soup recipe with tomato and tofu. It is fuss-free, super-speedy and can be made with simple and accessible ingredients. It also goes well with any kind of Japanese meal.

Ingredients

  • Kombu Dashi
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Silken/Soft Tofu
  • Miso of your choice

Steps

  1. Prepare the kombu dashi if you don’t have it. It’s very easy and quick to make from scratch.
  2. Add the cherry tomatoes and tofu and warm them up thoroughly.
  3. Add the miso of your choice and serve hot.
