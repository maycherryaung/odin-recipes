Vanilla Cake

Description

This is a classic butter cake that has been enhanced in texture and taste by special Japanese techniques. The cakes can be stored in the perfect condition for up to four days. You can use the same recipe for making cupcakes as well.

Ingredients

  • Plain/all purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Milk, full fat
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whip the eggs and sugar : Beat the eggs with sugar for 7 minutes untill thick and glossy, it changes from yellow to while, and tripled in volume.
  2. Gradually add flour : Whisk together the flour, baking powder amd salt. Then add into egg mixture in 3 lots, mixing for just 5 seconds on Speed 1 in between.
  3. Hot melted butter in milk : use a microwave or stove to heat milk with the butter till it melts.
  4. Mix some batter into hot milk
  5. Slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs : over 20 seconds on Speed 1. Scrape down sides of bowls, then mix for 10 seconds on Speed 1 - the batter should now be smooth.
  6. Pour into 2 cake pans lined with parchment/baking paper
  7. (Optional)Knock out big air bubbles by dropping the cake pans on the counter 3 times.
  8. Bake 30 minutes until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean.
  9. Cool upside down for level cakes on a cooling rack.
  10. Decorate! Frost with your favorite frosting, smoother with cream,fresh berries, swirl with chocolate fudge frosting or simply dust with icing sugar and serve with a dollop of yogurt or cream!
